Enchantment in the History of Capitalism

We’re a network of scholars interested in enchantment in the history of capitalism. Join us!

Home Roundtables Resources People Contact

We’re a network of scholars who seek to develop enchantment as an organizing theme in historical studies of capitalism. We hope to provide a platform for those interested in the historical role of enchantment as a tool, structure, or foundation for the organisation and the development of modern markets, economic institutions, and economic relationships.

We are currently holding a series of roundtables and recording our discussions in our memos. Also check out our Zotero folder for more resources.

If you’re keen to stay in the loop, join our mailing list or get in touch!
